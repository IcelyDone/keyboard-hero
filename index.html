How to Play

Hold down the 1-5 keys on your keyboard in correspondence to the notes traveling downwards on the page, and when they pass over the frets (numbered buttons), click the "Strum!" button with your mouse! If you successfully strum the note, you gain a point! If you fail to strum a note or mistime the strum, you lose HP!

You win when your points reach 50, or lose when your HP depletes to 0.
